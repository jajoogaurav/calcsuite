Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

At CalcSuite, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our website.

Information We Collect

CalcSuite is designed with privacy in mind. All financial calculations are performed entirely within your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your financial data to our servers.

Cookies

We may use essential cookies to ensure basic website functionality. Third-party services such as Google AdSense may also use cookies for advertising purposes. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

Third-Party Services

  • Google AdSense — We may display advertisements through Google AdSense. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your visit to our site and others. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings.
  • Chart.js — We use the Chart.js library (loaded from a CDN) for rendering charts. This is a client-side library and does not collect user data.

Data Security

Since all calculations happen in your browser and no personal data is collected by us, there is minimal security risk. We recommend keeping your browser and device updated for optimal security.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
