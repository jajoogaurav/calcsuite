About CalcSuite

CalcSuite is a free, open-source suite of financial calculators designed to help individuals make smarter money decisions. Whether you're planning a home loan, evaluating mutual fund investments, or comparing fixed deposit returns, our tools provide instant, accurate results.

Our Mission

We believe financial literacy starts with access to the right tools. Our mission is to provide everyone with easy-to-use, accurate financial calculators — completely free of charge, with no sign-up required.

What We Offer

  • EMI Calculator — Calculate monthly installments for home, car, or personal loans with detailed amortization schedules
  • SIP Calculator — Plan your mutual fund systematic investment plans and see compounding growth
  • Loan Prepayment Calculator — Discover how extra payments save you time and interest
  • Interest Calculator — Compare simple vs. compound interest across different frequencies
  • FD Calculator — Estimate fixed deposit maturity amounts and returns

Why Choose Us?

  • 🚀 Instant Results — Real-time calculations that update as you adjust inputs
  • 📊 Visual Charts — Beautiful charts to visualize your financial data
  • 📱 Mobile-Friendly — Works beautifully on all devices
  • 🔒 Privacy First — All calculations happen in your browser. We never store your financial data
  • 💯 100% Free — No hidden fees, no sign-ups, no limitations
